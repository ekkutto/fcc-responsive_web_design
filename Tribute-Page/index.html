Here is a timeline of Lionel Messi's life:

  • 24th June 1987:Lionel Andres Messi Cuccittini was born on Wednesday, June 24, 1987. He was born in Rosario, Argentina.
  • 31st March 1994:At the age of six, Messi became a member of the Old Boys. He played for this team for about six years. While he was at the youth club, he scored over 500 goals. The team was almost unbeatable.
  • 2nd February 2001:Messi and his family relocated to the city of Barcelona in Spain in February 2001. The family moved into an apartment that was very close to Barcelona's football team's stadium, Camp Nou.
  • 2nd February 2002:Messi became a student at Barcelona's youth academy, La Masia. In February 2002, he was officially enrolled in the Royal Spanish Football Federation (RFEF).
  • 17th October 2003:The footballer made his first appearance in a match against FC Porto.
  • 25th July 2005:On his 18th birthday, Messi landed his first contract as a senior team player. The contract was completed on June 24, 2005. The contract outlined that he would be an official player with the Barcelona football club until 2010. His buyout clause was set at a whopping €150 million.
  • 17th May 2006:Lionel Messi wins his first champions league on May 17, 2006.
  • 24th August 2008:Messi had the privilege of playing at the Football at the 2008 Summer Olympics which was held in Beijing and a number of other cities in the People's Republic of China. With Messi as a key player, Argentina won the tournament.
  • 27th May 2009:The final match of the 2009 UEFA Champions League was played on May 27, 2009. The match was held in Rome, Italy at the Stadio Olimpico. The winner of this match would be crowned the winners of the 2008-09 season of the UEFA Champions League. The winner of the match was Barcelona. They defeated Manchester United 2-0. Lionel Messi scored the final goal of the match. The UEFA Champions is a tournament that involves the top football clubs in all of Europe.
  • 1st December 2009:On December 1, 2009, Lionel Messi was awarded his very first Ballon d’Or.
  • 10th January 2011:Messi received his second consecutive Ballon d’Or on January 10, 2011. It was a great year for La Masia, the FC Barcelona academy. It became the first youth academy to have all three of the finalists for the Ballon d’Or in a single year. The players were Lionel Messi, Andres Iniesta, and Xavi.
  • 28th May 2011:The final match of the 2011 UEFA Champions League was held on May 28, 2011. The match was played at the Wembley Stadium in London. The match was to reveal who would be crowned the winner of the 2010-11 season of the UEFA Champions League. This was the 56th season of the tournament. It was also the 19th season in the Champions League era. The two teams that went head to head in this final match were Barcelona and Manchester United. Barcelona came out victorious and was dubbed the winners of the 2011 UEFA Champions League.
  • 9th January 2012:The award ceremony was held in Zurich on January 9, 2012. Lionel Messi received the title of world player of the year at the award ceremony. This marked the third consecutive year that Lionel Messi was named the winner of the Ballon d’Or.
  • 7th January 2014:Messi landed his fourth Ballon d’Or on January 7, 2013. The footballer was, for the fourth consecutive year, named the world’s best football player.
  • 13th July 2014Germany was defeated by Germany in the 2014 World Cup finals. Germany clinched a 1-0 victory.
  • 6th June 2015:On June 6, 2015, Lionel Messi secured his fourth Champions League.
  • 4th July 2015:The sizzling match ran into extra time. The match was decided by a penalty shootout. Chile defeated Argentina 4-1 on penalties.
  • 11th January 2016Messi’s skills as a footballer have not been going unnoticed. For the fifth time, Messi received the Ballon d’Or award.
  • 2nd December 2019:Messi attended the 64th annual ceremony of the Ballon d’Or. It was presented by France Football. Lionel Messi was recognized as the best player in the world for 2019. This marked the sixth time that Messi has received this award in his career.
  • 11th July 2021:Messi leads Argentina to their first Copa America title since 1993.
  • 10th August 2021:Messi completes a free transfer to Paris Saint Germain after his contract at Barcelona ran out.
  • 18th December 2022:Lionel Messi's career is complete. The 35-year-old Argentine led his country, as Diego Maradona did in 1986, to the world title. After extension, it was 3-3 and after penalty kicks, Argentina won.
